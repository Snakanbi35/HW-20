Welcome TO RAAD

Project Description: RAAD is a newly created app that me and other peers came up with. RAAD is a DIY app that allows users to find project instruction at home, it is meant to make finding DIYs easy for users.

During the process we made a Research plan we then follow that back up by making Interview plan and made our interviews. Following that we made surveys and it was from that survey we were able to jump into our User Insight, problem statement. We then discussed our Proto-persona and story board to see how our users find DIYs. We then used our competitor analysis to see what other apps and websites that had an idea like ours were doing, this helped us see how we could make our app more usable for DIY users. Then we begin to make a low-fi proto- type.
